API Documentation

"/getDataHour"

Use this to get home average temperature at a particular hour.

Example: http://127.0.0.1:5000/getDataHour?date=2019-01-20&hour=03

Provide Date and hour.

"/getDataDay"

Use this to get home average temperature at a particular day.

Example: http://127.0.0.1:5000/getDataDay?date=2019-01-20

Provide Date.

"/compareHour"

Use this to compare home average temperature at a particular hour of day with weather that day.

Example: http://127.0.0.1:5000/compareHour?date=2019-01-20&hour=07

Provide Date and Hour.

"/compareDay"

Use this to compare home average temperature at a particular day with weather that day.

Example: http://127.0.0.1:5000/compareDay?date=2019-01-20

Provide Date.
