API Documentation


"/getDataHour"


Use this to get home average temperature at a particular hour.


Example: http://127.0.0.1:5000/getDataHour?date=2019-01-20&hour=03


Provide Date and hour to get mean temperature of the house at that hour of the day.


"/getDataDay"


Use this to get home average temperature at a particular day.


Example: http://127.0.0.1:5000/getDataDay?date=2019-01-20


Provide Date to get mean temperature of the house at thats day.
